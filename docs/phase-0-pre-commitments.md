# Phase 0 pre-commitments — External Hire Chains
Dated 2026-07-20. Committed before any count was run.

## Input
The frozen External Hire Premium classification worksheet, copied
2026-07-20. 611 transitions, 453 internal, 158 external. No
re-classification. New rulings go to docs/decisions-log.md.

## Rule 1: the trigger
A spiral candidate is an external hire with tenure under 24 months.
Forced exits and underperformance exits count. Death, illness, and
planned bridge or retirement exits do not. Interim CEOs receive a
written ruling in the decision log before inclusion.

## Rule 2: the comparison group
Internal hires with tenure under 24 months are coded with the same
trigger rule. The study compares successor origin after failed
external hires against successor origin after failed internal hires.
A pattern that appears in both groups is failure-specific, not
external-specific, and will be reported as such.

## Rule 3: the kill threshold
The successor must fall inside the 2010-2022 window. Successors
split three ways: internal, boomerang, external. If any cell in the
external-trigger split is single-digit after coding, the study stops
and the null count is published on the methods page.

## Rule 4: the mechanism is a question
The deepening-lag pathway (failed external hire leaves the stock
lag intact or deeper, lag predicts the next external hire) is a
hypothesis this study tests. It is not a finding inherited from the
parent. The parent measured pre-hire lag as a selection condition
only.
